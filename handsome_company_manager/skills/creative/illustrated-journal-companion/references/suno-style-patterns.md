# Suno Style Patterns by Mood

Battle-tested Suno Style prompts grouped by the mood/arc they produce.
Each is a **starting template** — adapt 2-3 cues to the specific page.

## Format reminder

Suno's Style field accepts ~1,000 chars in v4.5+. Format that works:

```
[Mood] [genre], [emotion], [BPM], [2-4 instruments], [vocal persona?],
[production feel], [DYNAMIC ARC — describe the journey]
```

Two rules:
1. **Describe the journey, not just the genre.** "starts whisper, builds to
   hopeful chorus" beats "epic song" every time.
2. **3-5 instruments max.** More = mush. Pick the 2-3 that define the sound.

---

## Curiosity & Wonder (Mars, science, discoveries)

```
Children's storybook adventure, wonder and discovery, 88-95 BPM,
warm piano and glockenspiel, soft strings, light pizzicato,
gentle curiosity in delivery, building to hopeful major key,
acoustic, magical realism, Pixar-style innocence
```

Tweak:
- More "ancient mystery" → add "bamboo flute, deep bells"
- More "wide-eyed science" → add "kalimba, curious synth pulse, 8-bit bleeps"
- More "dreamy space" → add "celestial harp, music box, ambient pad"

---

## Playful Action (sports, games, recess, dance)

```
Upbeat playful pop, mischievous and joyful, 110-120 BPM,
hand claps, ukulele, tambourine, schoolyard fun energy,
cheerful whistling melody, bouncy rhythm,
sunny major key, innocent and carefree
```

Tweak:
- Sport/anthem → add "driving strings, brass hits, building percussion"
- Dance/rivalry → add "boom bap drums, funky bass, brass stabs"
- Quiet play → drop to "90 BPM, kalimba, brushed snare"

---

## Heroic & Ambitious (Olympic, big dreams, future goals)

```
Inspirational ballad, heroic but gentle, 92-100 BPM,
solo piano intro, building strings, steady heartbeat drums,
determined child narrator, warm and brave,
acoustic guitar arpeggios, motivational swell,
uplifting chorus, anthem for one dreamer
```

Tweak:
- Wide cinematic → add "full brass section, choir, marching snare"
- Personal dream → keep "solo piano, soft strings, intimate build"
- Patriotic feel → add "marching cadence, full brass, children's choir"

---

## Tender / Lullaby (moon, quiet moments, bedtime)

```
Tender piano lullaby with light orchestral swell, 70-80 BPM,
gentle child voice, contemplative wonder,
crystalline bell melody, soft strings, ambient space pad,
intimate and dreamy, lullaby feel,
narrow stereo, Ghibli-style innocence
```

Tweak:
- For moon/sky → add "celestial harp glissandos, twinkling stars melody"
- For parent-child → add "acoustic guitar strum, soft brushed drums"

---

## Tech / Future (robots, AI, autonomous cars, space)

```
Futuristic playful electronic, child-friendly synth pop, 100-115 BPM,
bouncy bass, sparkly arpeggios, soft vocoder echoes,
wonder and innovation, optimistic future feel,
retro 80s warmth blended with modern production,
innocent curiosity meets tomorrow
```

Tweak:
- Robot-specific → add "glitchy melodic hits, mechanical rhythm"
- Space/cosmic → add "ambient pad, sub-bass, distant signals"
- Car/transport → add "driving beat, motion synth, kinetic energy"

---

## Epic / Mythic / Ancient (门神, 神话, folk legends)

```
Chinese traditional epic folk, mythical guardian spirit, 85-95 BPM,
drums and gong, bamboo flute dizi, erhu melody,
heroic and majestic, ancient legend feeling,
child narrator voice over wide cinematic backdrop,
building to triumphant chorus
```

Tweak:
- More "ceremonial" → add "ritual bells, taiko-style drums"
- More "playful epic" → add "pizzicato strings, comedic woodwind"

## Daily Life Joy (fruit market, friends, family time)

```
Whimsical pop, bouncy and sweet, 90-100 BPM,
kalimba, marimba, light percussion, pizzicato strings,
playful call-and-response melody, sing-along chorus,
sunny morning feel, candy-colored major key,
ukulele, glockenspiel, market-day cheerfulness
```

Tweak:
- Friendship → add "whistling, two voices harmonizing, hand-clap rhythm"
- Family meal → add "warm acoustic guitar, soft brush drums, laughter"
- Outdoor play → add "breezy percussion, wind sounds (subtle)"

---

## Patriotic / Reverent (大国重器, military, national pride)

```
Patriotic children's anthem, heroic and proud, 90-100 BPM,
marching snare, full brass section, soaring strings,
military cadence softened for kids, brave and gentle,
choir of children's voices, hopeful and grand,
respectful tribute, wide stereo, lift-the-spirit feel,
"we are protected" — safety and pride
```

Tweak:
- Tribute/honor → drop BPM to 80, add "timpani, cello solo, restrained brass"
- Forward-looking → add "building synth layer, modern production warmth"

## Dynamic arc cheat sheet

When in doubt, paste one of these into your style description:

- "starts as a quiet whisper, builds to hopeful major key"
- "begins intimate, expands to full ensemble, then strips back to a single piano"
- "childlike curiosity → triumphant swell → tender whisper at the end"
- "steady heartbeat rhythm throughout, melody opens up in the second half"
- "playful bounce that briefly turns dramatic, then returns to joy"

These describe a PERFORMANCE, not a label — Suno uses them as a roadmap.
